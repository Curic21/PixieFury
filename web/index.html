Once upon a time...

In the Land of Departure, Terra and Aqua take an exam to achieve the Mark of Mastery, which will allow them to become Keyblade Masters.

However, only Aqua passes while Terra fails due to the abundant darkness in his heart. Shortly after the test, Master Xehanort disappears and a horde of creatures called the Unversed begin to appear throughout the worlds.

Ventus is coaxed by Xehanort's apprentice Vanitas into following Terra against Eraqu's wishes, while Eraqus orders Aqua to watch over Terra and bring Ventus back to the Land of Departure.
